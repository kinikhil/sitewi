About Us

NIMAS, ranked as one of  the best institutes in Kolkata, is offering undergraduate & post graduate programs that is accredited by Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, MAKAUT formerly known as WBUT.

A highly ranked institute with a global outlook, we’re deeply connected to our community and value our place in it. Our 23 years of academic excellence, innovative teaching and learning practices, along with our strong ties to industry, make us one of the leading providers of higher education in India.
