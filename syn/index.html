Q. What exactly is General Pediatrics?

General pediatrics is the branch of medical science dedicated exclusively to the physical, functional, and therapeutic medical care of infants, children, and growing adolescents, from birth up to the age of 18. A doctor specialized in this area is a pediatrician. At Synergy Hospital, we interface seamlessly with primary pediatricians to manage cases requiring advanced pediatric surgical or specialized structural urological repair.
